HUB + AI

Add as many or few smart devices as you want, choreograph them to work with ZOE HUB & AI , and create a smarter home that automatically reacts as life happens.

Enhance your devices to the next level.

ZOE AI adds an AI layer to your different smart home devices, gathers data of wearables, smartphones, and IoT devices. This information is end-to-end encrypted and sent to our servers to be processed in real time understanding situations and the environment.

The processed data is returned automatically adapted to the involved devices. In other words, ZOE AI knows what do you want at any moment. As long as ZOE AI lives within your home, it learns about your habits and your lifestyle.

Whether you have two smart devices or 200, all you need is ZOE HUB to create a smart home. Like a live-in translator, the HUB communicates with all of your different connected products–regardless of their wireless protocol–so that you can easily monitor and control them from the ZOE app.

ZOE HUB contains ZigBee, Bluetooth and WiFi radios. There's no hard-wiring or installation needed. Simply connect it to your Internet router using the included Ethernet cable or through WiFi, attach the power cord to the back of the HUB, and you're ready to start creating a safer, more efficient smarter home.
